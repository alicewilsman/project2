Mim’s is a small, locally owned restaruant serving a variety of mediterranian and American food. Located in the Univeristy of Minnesota, St. Paul neighborhood, Mim’s serves students and faculty as well as locals to the area.

Tasteful and affordable, Mim’s serves high quality food. Attatched to Lori’s coffee shop, the building offeres a warm, friendly environment good both for socializing, studying, or relaxing.

Mim’s offers dining-in, as well as catering options. Be sure to stop by, or call if you have a questions!
